Renderman rendering passes.
Just about any scene can be broken down into multiple passes to render: a shadow pass for shadows (deep or not), a txmake pass for textures, and so on, all leading to the final pass for your fully rendered image. Most of these passes are pre-defined and happen "behind the scenes" without requiring you to do anything - you set up your scene and RenderMan does the rest - but you can also take advantage of passes by creating them yourself.
